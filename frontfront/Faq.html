  Frequently Asked Questions (FAQS)

What is MedCheck ?

MedCheck is a mobile and web-based platform that allows you to verify the authencity of drugs,food,drinks and cosmetic products using a barcode scan or NAFDAC number

How does MedCheck verify a product?

MedCheck connects to official product databases(e.g NAFDAC) and uses batch information to check i a product is registered,authentic,expired or flagged as unsafe.

Do i need to register before using MedCheck?

NO. You can verify products without an accont.However,Creating an account lets you save your history,report suspicious item and receive customs alerts.

Can I use MedCheck without internet access?

Some features(like scanning or manual input) may work offline but verification requires an internet connection to check the central database in real time.

What types of products can i check?

  • Drugs & Medication
  • Packaged foods and drinks
  • Cosmetic & skincare products
  • Supplements and herbal remedies

How do I report a fake product or seller?

On the app, use the "Report" button after scanning or searching a product.You can also include the store name,location and photo evidence if available

Is MedCheck approved by NAFDAC or other health bodies?

MedCheck is built to work in alignment with regulatory bodies like NAFDAC. We continously collaborate with public health stakeholders to ensure data accuracy and legal compliance.

Can MedCheck help pharmacies or store too?

Absolutely! Retailers and pharmacists can use MedCheck to verify inventory,avoid stocking fake goods and gain consumer trust through verified transparency.
