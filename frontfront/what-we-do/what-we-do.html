What We Do

At MedCheck, we're a the mission to protect you from counterfeit, expired, and unregistered products.

Our platform empowers everyday consumers to make safer, smarter choices — right from their phone.

Product Verification

Scan the barcode or enter the NAFDAC registration number to instantly check authenticity and approval status of drugs, foods, drinks, and cosmetics.

Verify Product

Product Reporting

Found a suspicious item or seller? Report it directly through MedCheck.

We forward verified reports to relevant authorities and alert the community.

Report Product

Public Awareness

We keep you informed with real-time alerts on flagged products, safety warnings, and educational tips to help avoid harmful goods.

Vision Statement

To become the leading digital platform in Africa for eliminating counterfeit products, fostering a world where every consumer can make safe, informed choices — confidently, instantly, and securely.

Mission Statement

To empower consumers with simple, reliable tools to verify the authenticity of drugs, foods, drinks, and cosmetics before purchase, helping prevent harm and saving lives through technology, transparency, and awareness.

Core Value

We aim to build and maintain trust between consumers, producers, and regulators by ensuring transparent, accurate product verification.
